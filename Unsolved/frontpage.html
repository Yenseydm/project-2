TIP #1: Keep your children learning without them knowing!

Kids tend to not want to learn when you hand them so many worksheets. Make it fun! Trick them in learning by going to museums where they can learn about art or history. Let them pay at the store or even bake where it shows them Mathematics!

Last updated 3 mins ago

TIP #2: READ!

Reading helps your little ones even if you think they do not understand. Wether it's a nighttime ritual or a daytime duty, reading will improve your child's vocabulary. Start off with simple books and show them the pictures as you go through it. And teach your child not only how to read but what is a book!
